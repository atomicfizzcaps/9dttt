How to Play Backgammon

  • Objective: Move all 15 of your checkers into your home board and then bear them off.
  • Setup: Each player starts with 15 checkers positioned on the board.
  • Rolling: Roll two dice to determine how many points you can move.
  • Movement: Move your checkers forward according to the dice values. Brown moves clockwise (24‚Üí1), White moves counter-clockwise (1‚Üí24).
  • Doubles: If you roll doubles, you get to use that number four times!
  • Hitting: If you land on a point with a single opponent checker, it's sent to the bar.
  • Re-entering: Checkers on the bar must re-enter before other moves can be made.
  • Bearing Off: Once all your checkers are in your home board (points 1-6 for Brown, 19-24 for White), you can start bearing them off.
  • Winning: First player to bear off all 15 checkers wins!
