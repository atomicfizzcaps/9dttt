How to Play Connect Four

  • Objective: Connect 4 of your discs in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally)!
  • Taking Turns: Players take turns dropping one disc into any column.
  • Dropping Discs: Click on a column to drop your disc. It falls to the lowest empty spot.
  • Strategy: Block your opponent while building your own connections!
  • Winning: First player to connect 4 in a row wins the round!
  • Draw: If the board fills up with no winner, it's a draw.
