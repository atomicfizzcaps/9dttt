How to Play Memory Game

  • Objective: Find all matching pairs of cards in the fewest moves possible!
  • Gameplay: Click or tap on a card to flip it over and reveal the symbol.
  • Matching: Flip two cards per turn. If they match, they stay face up!
  • No Match: If the cards don't match, they flip back face down.
  • Memory: Try to remember where each symbol is located.
  • Winning: Match all pairs to win! Try to do it in the fewest moves.
  • Keyboard: Use Tab to navigate cards, Enter/Space to flip.
