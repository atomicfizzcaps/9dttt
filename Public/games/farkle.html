How to Play Farkle

  • Objective: Be the first player to reach 10,000 points!
  • üéØ 1000 to Start: You must score at least 1000 points in a SINGLE TURN before you can start banking points. Until then, any points you score are lost at the end of your turn!
  • Rolling: Roll all 6 dice. You MUST set aside at least one scoring die each roll.
  • Scoring Dice: Click on dice to select them for scoring. Only valid scoring combinations count!
  • Continue or Bank: After selecting scoring dice, you can roll the remaining dice for more points, or bank your turn score.
  • Hot Dice: If all 6 dice score, you get "hot dice" and can roll all 6 again!
  • Farkle: If you roll and NO dice score, you "Farkle" and lose ALL points from that turn!
  • Strategy: The more you push your luck, the more you can score... but risk losing it all!
  • Winning: First player to 10,000 points wins! (Other players get one more turn to try to beat the score.)
