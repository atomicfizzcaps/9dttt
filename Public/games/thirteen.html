  • Valid Plays:
    • Single - One card, beat with higher card
    • Pair - Two cards of same rank
    • Triple - Three cards of same rank
    • Straight - 5 consecutive cards (A-2 NOT valid!)
    • Flush - 5 cards of same suit
    • Full House - Triple + Pair
    • Four of a Kind - 4 cards of same rank + 1
    • Straight Flush - Straight + Flush (highest!)
  • Special Rule: 5-card combos can beat a single 2!
  • Passing: If you can't or don't want to play, you must pass.
  • New Round: When opponent passes, you start fresh.
  • Winning: First player to play all their cards wins!
