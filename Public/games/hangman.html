How to Play Hangman

  • Objective: Guess the hidden word before the hangman is complete!
  • Guessing: Click letters on the keyboard to guess them.
  • Correct Guess: The letter appears in the word!
  • Wrong Guess: A body part is added to the hangman (6 total).
  • Hints: Use the hint button if you're stuck (costs 1 try).
  • Winning: Reveal the complete word to win!
  • Losing: If the hangman is complete before you guess the word, you lose.
  • Streak: Build a winning streak for bragging rights!
