How to Play Crystal Connect

  • Objective: Match 3 or more crystals of the same color to score points!
  • Matching: Click a crystal, then click an adjacent crystal to swap them. Swaps only work if they create a match.
  • Chain Reactions: When crystals match and disappear, new crystals fall down and may create more matches!
  • Combos: Chain reactions multiply your score - the longer the chain, the bigger the bonus!
  • Special Crystals: Match 4+ crystals to create special crystals with explosive powers!
  • Level Goal: Reach the target score before running out of moves to advance!
  • Power-Ups: Earn power-ups by creating big matches. Use them strategically!
  • Keyboard: Arrow keys to move selection, Space/Enter to select, H for hint.
